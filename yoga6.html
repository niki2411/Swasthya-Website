Yoga Pose:Virabhadrasana II (Warrior II)

Instructions:

  • From Tadasana, exhale and step your feet 4-5 feet apart.
  • Turn your right foot 90° out, left foot slightly in (about 10°).
  • Exhale, bend your right knee so the thigh is parallel to the floor, knee over heel.
  • Keep your left leg straight, lift the left hip, and open the chest.
  • Rotate knees outward, lift ribcage, and draw the tailbone down.
  • Inhale, extend arms to shoulder height, gaze over your right hand.
  • Stretch through the fingertips, keeping shoulders relaxed.

Benefits:

  • Increases mobility and flexibility in the hips.
  • Tones and strengthens the lower back, legs, thighs, core, arms, and abdomen.
  • Promotes correct spinal functioning
  • Improves balance.
  • Builds stamina and inner strength.
