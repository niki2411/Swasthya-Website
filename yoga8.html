Yoga Pose:Vasisthasana (Side Plank Pose)

Instructions:

  • From Plank Pose, exhale and shift your weight onto your left hand and the outer edge of your left foot.
  • Keep your torso strong and lifted as you stack your right foot on top of the left.
  • Inhale to find your balance. Exhale, then reach your right arm straight up toward the sky.
  • Inhale again, lengthening through the body from feet to head.
  • Exhale, press firmly through your supporting hand, engage your legs, roll your shoulders back, and open your chest.
  • Keep the spine long and strong.
  • Hold for a few breaths, then gently return to Plank and repeat on the other side.

Benefits:

  • Strengthens the abdomen, arms, and legs.
  • Improves balance of the body.
