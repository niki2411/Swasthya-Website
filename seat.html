Yoga Pose: Paschimottanasana

Paschimottanasana (Seated Forward Bend) is a calming pose that deeply stretches the back body and helps soothe the mind. It's excellent for flexibility and inner peace.

Instructions:

  • Sit with your legs extended straight in front of you.
  • Inhale, lengthen your spine, and raise your arms overhead.
  • Exhale as you fold forward from the hips, reaching for your feet or shins.
  • Keep your spine long and avoid rounding the back.
  • Hold the pose for 30 seconds to 1 minute, breathing gently.

Benefits:

  • Stretches the spine, shoulders, and hamstrings.
  • Stimulates the liver, kidneys, ovaries, and uterus.
  • Helps relieve stress, anxiety, and mild depression.
  • Improves digestion and calms the mind.
