Yoga Pose: Marjaryasana (Cat Stretch)

Marjaryasana, or Cat Stretch Pose, is a gentle flow between two poses that warms the body and brings flexibility to the spine. It is often used as a warm-up in yoga sessions.

Instructions:

  • Start on your hands and knees in a tabletop position.
  • Ensure your knees are directly below your hips and your wrists below your shoulders.
  • Inhale, lifting your tailbone and head up as your belly drops toward the floor (Cow Pose).
  • Exhale, rounding your spine upward and tucking your chin to your chest (Cat Pose).
  • Repeat the motion for 5-10 breaths, synchronizing with your breath.

Benefits:

  • Improves spinal flexibility and mobility.
  • Stretches the back and neck gently.
  • Relieves tension in the spine and shoulders.
  • Promotes emotional balance and stress relief.
