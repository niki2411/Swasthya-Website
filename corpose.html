Yoga Pose: Savasana (Corpse Pose)

Savasana is a resting pose typically practiced at the end of a yoga session. It allows the body to absorb the benefits of the practice, bringing deep relaxation to the body and mind.

Instructions:

  • Lie flat on your back with legs slightly apart and arms at your sides.
  • Let your feet fall naturally outward and palms face upward.
  • Close your eyes and take slow, deep breaths.
  • Focus on relaxing every part of your body from head to toe.
