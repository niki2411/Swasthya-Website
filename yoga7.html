Yoga Pose:Salabhasana (Locust Pose)

Instructions:

  • Lie on your belly, arms alongside your body, palms facing down.
  • Inhale and lift your legs and upper torso off the floor.
  • Keep knees straight and legs extended upward.
  • Balance your weight on the pelvis, belly, and lower ribs.
  • Hold the pose for a few breaths, keeping your body engaged.
  • Exhale and slowly lower your legs and head to the floor.
  • Relax and repeat 2-3 times.

Benefits:

  • Reduces abdominal fat and tones the core.
  • Expands the chest and improves lung capacity.
  • Enhances spinal flexibility and alignment
  • Stimulates digestion and boosts energy.
  • Strengthens back muscles.
