oga Pose: Makarasana (Crocodile Pose)

Makarasana is a relaxing posture that resembles a crocodile resting in water. It is primarily practiced at the end of a yoga session to restore the body and calm the nervous system.

Instructions:

  • Lie flat on your stomach with legs extended and slightly apart.
  • Fold your arms and rest your head on your hands, turning your face to one side.
  • Close your eyes and allow your entire body to relax into the floor.
  • Breathe slowly and deeply, letting go of all tension with each exhalation.

Benefits:

  • Deeply relaxes the body and mind.
  • Relieves fatigue and stress.
  • Helps regulate blood pressure.
  • Soothes the nervous system and promotes better sleep.
