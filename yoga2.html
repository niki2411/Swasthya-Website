Yoga Pose:Apanasana (Knee to chest)

Instructions:

  • Lie on your stomach in a relaxed position. Place your hands crossed in front and put your head on them.
  • Lie back on your back with bent knees and flat feet on the floor.
  • Draw your knees up toward your chest, grasping your shins or the back of your thighs.
  • Relax your back and head on the ground and maintain the position for 20-30 seconds, taking deep breath.
  • Release the pose by slowly lowering your knees to the initial position.

Benefits:

  • This pose will help massage the digestive organs and relax discomfort and bloating.
  • The posture calms the mind and minimizes anxiety and stress.
  • Apanasana relaxes the muscles at the back of the lower back and hips, relaxing and easing tension.
  • The twisting and stretching of the spine may enhance breathing by opening up the chest and lungs.
