Yoga Pose: Navakasana (Boat Pose)

Navakasana or Boat Pose is a core-strengthening yoga posture that challenges the abdominal muscles while improving balance and posture.

Instructions:

  • Sit on the mat with legs extended and spine straight.
  • Place your hands behind your thighs, lift your feet off the floor.
  • Balance on your sit bones, bringing your shins parallel to the floor.
  • Extend your arms forward, parallel to the floor, palms facing in.
  • Engage your core and lengthen your spine. Hold the position.
  • To advance, straighten your legs into a 'V' shape.

Benefits:

  • Strengthens core muscles, hip flexors, and spine.
  • Improves balance, concentration, and posture.
  • Stimulates kidneys, thyroid and intestines.
