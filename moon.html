Yoga Flow: Chandra Namaskar

Chandra Namaskar (Moon Salutation) is a calming and grounding sequence that honors the feminine, lunar energy. It is often practiced in the evening to relax the body and mind.

Instructions:

  • Begin in Tadasana (Mountain Pose), standing tall and centered.
  • Inhale and raise your arms overhead into a crescent moon shape.
  • Exhale and fold forward into a gentle forward bend.
  • Move through side stretches, low lunges, and a gentle backbend.
  • Repeat on both sides to complete the flow symmetrically.
  • Finish in a seated or standing meditative posture, focusing on breath.

Benefits:

  • Promotes emotional balance and calmness.
  • Improves flexibility in hips, legs, and spine.
  • Supports reproductive and digestive health.
  • Balances energy and aids restful sleep.
