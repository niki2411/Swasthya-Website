Yoga Pose:Virabhadrasana I (Warrior I)

Instructions:

  • Begin in Mountain Pose, and then step feet 4-5 feet wide.
  • Inhale, stretch arms up, palms facing palm to palm.
  • Turn right foot 90° out, and left foot 60° in.
  • Exhale, shift hips and body to face the right foot.
  • Inhale, lift the left arch and push left heel down.
  • Exhale, bend the right knee over heel, shin being vertical.
  • Straighten and activate the left leg.
  • Distribute your weight evenly between both legs and stay grounded through the back foot

Benefits:

  • Strengthens the legs, ankles, arms, and shoulders.
  • Stretches the chest, lungs, abdomen, and hip flexors.
  • Improves balance and stability through grounding and alignment.
  • Enhances flexibility in the hips and spine.
