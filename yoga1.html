Yoga Pose:Shavasana (corpse pose)

Instructions:

  • Lie on your back with relaxed arms around 15 cm away from the body, palms up.
  • Support a thin pillow or folded cloth under the head if necessary for comfort.
  • Allow the fingers to naturally curl and the feet to slightly fall apart into a relaxed state.
  • Maintain the head and spine aligned; keep the head centered.
  • Close the eyes and relax the entire body, holding completely still.
  • Bring awareness to your natural breath have it become calm, deep, and rhythmic.
  • Gradually, after a few minutes, bring awareness back to your body and environment.
  • Gradually release the posture with slow, mindful movements.

Benefits:

  • Deeply relaxes the entire body and mind.
  • Ideal before sleep, after dynamic practices like Surya Namaskara, and whenever feeling tired
  • Enhances body awareness and calms the nervous system.
  • Supports recovery and balances energy after yoga practice.
